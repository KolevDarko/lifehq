Everyone needs blocks of uninterrupted time for deep productive work. That is when the real work gets done.

The best way to get focused work done consistently is to work in fixed focus periods. Those are much easier to protect against distractions.
No matter how important you are, the world can survive for 25 minutes without your attention.

Advanced Pomodoro module

The Pomodoro technique specifies short work periods, usually 25 mins, for focused work, with 5 minute breaks in-between.
However LifeHQ allows you to specify custom work and break periods for each session.

I work in 45/10 minute cycles for programming work and 25/5 cycles for marketing and admin stuff at LifeHQ.
It's nothing special until you take it seriously, just like everything in life.

The magic happens when you prepare the right way.
At LifeHQ I've added specific preparation and conclusion questions to get you closer to achieving flow even before you start.

Before you start answer these short questions so your mind starts working towards solution before starting.

  • What are you working on?
  • How will you get started?
  • Possible blockers and how to handle them.

Check yourself at the end of each cycle to detect tiredness, procrastination or simply trying to do too much at once.
