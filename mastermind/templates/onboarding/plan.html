The daily master list

The LifeHQ daily master list is split into three sections. Derived from the Eisenhower priority matrix

  • Urgent - Tasks that must get done today
  • Important - Non urgent tasks that move you closer to your goals.
  • Extra - For everything else. Non urgent and non important things.

For the long term planners we also have weekly planner.

The weekly planner lets you schedule tasks the important tasks full week in advance.
This gets automatically synced with your daily list on midnight.

Separating the planning phase and the working phase will easily save you 1 hour in the morning.
